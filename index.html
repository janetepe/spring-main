Shades of Spring

In the temperate regions of the world, spring is one of the four distinct seasons of the year. It comes immediately after winter but precedes summer. It falls in the months of March, April and May depending on the country and how far it is from the tropics in the northern hemisphere and in the southern hemisphere, spring months are September, October and November.

It is the season with natural beauty and different shades of colors. It is the time when green vegetation blosoms with flowers before the actual leaves. If you are a lover of natural beauty, it is a time of the year you see mixture of colors that only nature can blend
